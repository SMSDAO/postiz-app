# AI Poster Application - User Guide

## Welcome to AI Poster! 🎨

AI Poster is an intelligent social media content creation and scheduling platform that helps you generate, manage, and publish AI-powered posts across multiple social media platforms.

## Getting Started

### Accessing Your Dashboard

1. **Login**: Navigate to your AI Poster user dashboard at `http://localhost:4300`
2. **First Time Setup**: Complete your profile and connect your first social media account
3. **Dashboard Overview**: Familiarize yourself with the main dashboard sections

### Dashboard Overview

The user dashboard provides four key metrics:

- **Total Posts**: Count of all your created posts
- **Scheduled**: Number of posts ready to be published
- **Platforms**: Connected social media accounts
- **Engagement**: Total interactions across all your posts

## Quick Actions

### 1. Generate AI Post ✨
- Click "Generate AI Post" button
- Enter topic or keywords
- AI creates engaging content
- Review and customize

### 2. Schedule Post 📅
- Select date and time
- Choose target platforms
- Set up recurring posts

### 3. Connect Platform 🔗
- Add social media accounts
- Supported: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, Reddit, Mastodon, Bluesky

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Cannot Connect Social Media Account
1. Check internet connection
2. Verify login credentials
3. Try reconnecting
4. Clear browser cache

#### Posts Not Publishing
1. Verify account connection
2. Check scheduled time
3. Ensure content meets platform requirements
